Fried Mozzarella Cheese Sticks

Description

Mozzarella sticks are very easy to make at home. They're deep-fried until golden and crispy on the outside, with melted gooey cheese on the inside. They're so good and take just minutes to fry! Try dipping them in a marinara sauce!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 1/2 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 tsp garlic salt
  • 2/3 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup of cornstarch
  • 2 cups of oil for frying, or as needed
  • 1 (16 ounce) package mozzarella cheese sticks

Steps

  1. Whisk water and eggs together in a small bowl.
  2. Mix bread crumbs and garlic salt together in a medium bowl.
  3. Blend flour and cornstarch together in a third bowl.
  4. Heat oil to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C) in a large, heavy saucepan.
  5. Dredge a mozzarella stick in flour; shake off excess. Dip into egg mixture. Lift up so excess egg drips back in the bowl. Press into bread crumbs to coat. Place breaded mozzarella stick on a plate or wire rack. Repeat with remaining mozzarella sticks.
  6. Use a spider spoon or a pair of tongs to lower 3 to 4 mozzarella sticks into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown, about 30 seconds. Remove from heat and drain on paper towels. Repeat to fry remaining mozzarella sticks.
